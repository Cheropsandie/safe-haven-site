Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What services do you offer?

We provide home sitting and pet sitting services while you are away. This includes caring for your pets, checking on your home, watering plants, basic cleaning, and ensuring everything remains safe and secure.

2. Do you stay at the home or visit daily?

This depends on your needs. We can stay at your home or make scheduled daily visits. We discuss and agree on the arrangement before the service begins.

3. What types of pets do you care for?

We care for common household pets such as dogs, cats, birds, and small animals. If you have special requirements, feel free to discuss them with us.

4. Are my home and pets safe with you?

Yes. We take trust and responsibility very seriously. Your home and pets are treated with care, respect, and confidentiality at all times.

We currently serve clients within our agreed service areas. Please contact us to confirm availability in your location.

9. How is pricing determined?

Pricing depends on the duration of service, number of pets, and specific care requirements. We provide clear pricing after discussing your needs.
